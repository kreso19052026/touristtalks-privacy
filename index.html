TouristTalks – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

TouristTalks does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app works completely offline and makes no network requests.

Data Collection

We collect no personal data whatsoever. All information you enter in the app (your name, avatar, visited countries, wishlist, checklist items) is stored exclusively on your device and is never sent anywhere.

Internet Access

The app does not use the internet. It requires no internet connection to function. All country data, phrases, and maps are bundled inside the app itself.

Third-Party Services

The optional "Support" button opens an external PayPal link in your device's browser. Any data you provide to PayPal at that point is governed by PayPal's own privacy policy. TouristTalks itself sends nothing to PayPal.

Permissions

The app requests no device permissions.

Children

This app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
